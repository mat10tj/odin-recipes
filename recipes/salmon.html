Baked salmon with chopped tomato and green onions

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) salmon fillet, halved
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 5 green onions, chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place salmon on oiled sheet pan or shallow baking dish and fold the outer edges under the salmon.
  3. add chopped tomatoes and green onions then season with salt and pepper.
  4. cook salmon in preheated oven, uncovered, until fish flakes easily with a fork, for 20 mins.
